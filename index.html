Welcome to Boribuz

Located in the heart of Scarborough, Boribuz is your destination for authentic Bangladeshi cuisine. Our name, a playful take on the Bengali word for "feast," reflects our commitment to bringing you a delightful dining experience. We use traditional recipes and the freshest ingredients to create dishes that are both familiar and exciting. Join us for a meal and become part of the Boribuz family.
